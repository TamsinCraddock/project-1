Our Values

Charity & Giving Back

We are passionate about giving back to the community.


We donate a portion of our proceeds to local pet charities each month. Each member of our team also dedicates time to volunteer at local shelters and rescue organisations.

Qualified Pet Care Specialists

All of our pet care specialists are fully qualified in animal care and wellbeing. We have also certified each of our pet care specialists to be fully Garda Vetted and background checked.


This ensures that you can have peace of mind knwing that your pet is in the best hands.

100% Transparency

We think it's a pretty big deal that we have access to your house and are responsible for your pets. That's why we are 100% transparent.


All services are time stamped and GPS tracked. Clients receive real-time notifications when services are complete along with a service summary.

Our Services

Dog Walking

Whether you're looking to have your dog walked while you're at work or if you just need some help taking those daily dog walks off your plate. We're here to help!


We walk in small groups - never large packs.

House Sitting

We take care of things while you're away. This service includes feeding and visiting your beloved pets, making sure they have plenty of fresh water and a clean area to relax in (this includes cleaning litter and doggy waste from your home or garden).


One of our dedicated pet specialists will stay with your pet for 30 minutes to ensure that they are given some one-to-one TLC for the day.

Cat Visits

We visit your cat while you're at work or out of the house for extended periods of time. Cat visits include clean litter box, food, water, meds if needed and a 15 minute dedicated play time or cuddle.


Choose from morning and/or evening visits or a daily anytime visit.

Expert Dog Walking Service:

Reliable, flexible dog walking services for the busy professional.

Exercise is an important part of your dog's day. Happy Tails provides dog walks in multiple options from group walks to one-on-one sessions and our dog walking services can be provided on a set weekly schedule or on an as needed basis. During a dog walk we come to your home to collect your dog and drop them back home once the walk is completed. Time can be utilized for a leashed walk, yard play, beach run or any combination.
