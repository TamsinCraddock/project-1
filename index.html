Our Values

Charity & Giving Back

We are passionate about giving back to the community.


We donate a portion of our proceeds to local pet charities each month. Each member of our team also dedicates time to volunteer at local shelters and rescue organisations.

Qualified Pet Care Specialists

All of our pet care specialists are fully qualified in animal care and wellbeing. We have also certified each of our pet care specialists to be fully Garda Vetted and background checked.


This ensures that you can have peace of mind knwing that your pet is in the best hands.

100% Transparency

We think it's a pretty big deal that we have access to your house and are responsible for your pets. That's why we are 100% transparent.


All services are time stamped and GPS tracked. Clients receive real-time notifications when services are complete along with a service summary.
